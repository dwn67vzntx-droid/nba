Timeline

1946–1960s

Origins and consolidation after the BAA–NBL merger. Early stars include George Mikan and Bob Cousy.

1970s–1980s

League stabilization, expansion, and the rise of Magic Johnson and Larry Bird who revitalized interest.

1990s

Michael Jordan era: global explosion of the NBA brand, increased merchandising, and an international influx of talent.

2000s–Present

Modern evolution: pace-and-space offenses, the three-point revolution, analytics, and player empowerment.
