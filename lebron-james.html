LeBron James

Teams: Cleveland Cavaliers, Miami Heat, Los Angeles Lakers

Major Accomplishments: 4× NBA champion, 4× NBA MVP, 19+ All-Star selections (active), multiple Finals appearances across three decades, widely regarded as one of the most versatile players in history.

Career Snapshot

LeBron ranks among the all-time leaders in points, assists, and is the NBA's all-time scoring leader (as of recent seasons). He combines scoring, playmaking, and longevity at an elite level.

Notable Awards

  • MVPs: 2009, 2010, 2012, 2013
  • NBA championships: 2012, 2013, 2016, 2020
  • All-NBA and All-Defensive team selections across multiple seasons
